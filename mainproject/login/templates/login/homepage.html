About

For the past 10 years WE has been actively involved with the local community, looking after and representing the interests of local residents.

Permanent residents and tenants who live in our locality are eligible for membership.

Each year an annual meeting is conducted to select a working committee for the succeeding year.

The action council includes 17 members including the President, Vice President, Secretary, Joint Secretary, and Treasurer.
